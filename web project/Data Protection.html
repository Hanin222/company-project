Privacy Statement

Use of our site is generally possible without providing personal data. The use of individual services from our site may result in differing regulations; these cases are explained separately below. Your personal data (e.g. name, address, email address, telephone number, etc.) will be processed by us in accordance with German data protection laws, Data is thus considered personal if it can be clearly assigned to an individual Data Protection Act (BDSG) and the Telemedia Act (TMG). The following regulations inform you about the nature and scope of the personal data we collect, process, and use, and for what purpose. Please note that internet-based data transmission has security gaps and that complete protection against access by third parties is impossible.

Cookies

For our part, we use so-called cookies to recognize the multiple use of our service by the same user/internet connection owner. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer and saved by your browser. They are used to optimize our website and services. These are typically referred to as so-called “session cookies”, which are tain information that allows our servers to recognize you when you return to our site. This is based on the IP address data stored in the cookie. The information obtained in this way is used to improve our services and to allow you easier access to our site. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however, please be advised that if you opt out of using cookies, you may not be able to use all the features of this website.

Server data

For technical reasons, the following data sent by your internet browser to us or to our webspace provider will be collected in server log files:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Website from which you visit us (Referrer URL)
  • Web page you are visiting
  • Date and time of your access
  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) our services.
This anonymous data is stored separately from any personal data you may submit and will not be traced back to a specific person. It is used for statistical purposes to improve our website and our services.

Contact Options

On our site, we offer the possibility to contact us by e-mail and/or via a contact form. In this case, the information provided by the user is stored for the purpose of processing his or her contact request. This data is not transferred to third parties. We will not compare the data collected during newsletter registration with any other data that might be collected by other components of our site.

Use of PIWIK web analytics

We use the web analysis service, PIWIK, on our website. Piwik uses cookies for this analysis. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer that enable analysis of how you use our website. The information generated by cookies, such as the time, place, and frequency of your visit to the website, including yours is anonymized, so that you as a user will remain anonymous to us. The information generated by the cookies about your use of this website will not be disclosed to third parties. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however, please be advised that if you opt out of using cookies, you may not be able to use all the features of this website.

Information/revocation/deletion

If you have any questions regarding the collection, processing, or use of your personal information, or to request that your data be disclosed, corrected, blocked, or deleted, or to revoke consent, please contact us.
